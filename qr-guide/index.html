Complete QR Code Guide

QR codes (Quick Response codes) are two-dimensional barcodes that store data such as URLs, text, contact details, and more. They are widely used in marketing, payments, events, and business operations.

How QR Codes Work

When scanned with a smartphone camera, the encoded data is instantly decoded and redirected to its destination, such as a website or app.

Types of QR Codes

Static QR codes contain fixed data, while dynamic QR codes allow editing the destination without changing the printed code.

Best Practices

Use high contrast colors, proper size, and test the QR code before publishing it publicly.
